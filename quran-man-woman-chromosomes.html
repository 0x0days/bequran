Pattern 4b · The Chromosome Connection

The Quran says رَجُل 23 times and ٱمْرَأَة 23 times. A father contributes exactly 23 chromosomes. A mother contributes exactly 23 chromosomes.

This page is about the biology. The number 23 is not arbitrary it is the precise contribution of each parent to every human cell. This was unknown when the Quran was written. It was confirmed 1,323 years later using microscopy that didn't exist until the 20th century.

Confirmed by Tjio & Levan, 1955
