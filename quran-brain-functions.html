The Quran named the exact region of the brain where lying is planned — 1,400 years before neuroscience existed.

96:15–16 — Surah Al-ʿAlaq · The Clot
كَلَّا لَئِن لَّمْ يَنتَهِ لَنَسْفَعًا بِٱلنَّاصِيَةِ ۝ نَاصِيَةٍ كَاذِبَةٍ خَاطِئَةٍ
"No! If he does not desist, We will surely drag him by the forelock — a lying, sinful forelock."

For centuries, scholars assumed the forehead was the seat of vision — it seemed logical, since eyes sit just below it. But the Quran called that same region lying and sinful. Modern neuroscience confirms: the prefrontal cortex — located directly behind the forehead — is where the brain plans deception.
