Fastest Drinkers

The Quran compares the drinking of people in Hell to thirsty camels. Only recently did science discover why that comparison is precise camels are the fastest drinkers on Earth.

56:54-55 · Surah Al-Waqi'ah
فَشَارِبُونَ عَلَيْهِ مِنَ الْحَمِيمِ فَشَارِبُونَ شُرْبَ الْهِيمِ
"They will drink on top of it boiling water. Drinking the drink of thirsty camels."
The Discovery
The word الهِيمِ (al-haym) specifically means extremely thirsty camels. A 600 kg camel can drink 200 liters of water in just three minutes the fastest drinking rate of any mammal. This was unknown in the 7th century.

The Quran chose the fastest drinker on Earth.

The word الهِيمِ refers to camels driven to extreme thirst, capable of consuming 200 liters in just three minutes a rate no other mammal matches. The verse could have said "drinking of the thirsty" and still been true. Instead it specified camels the one animal whose drinking speed is unmatched. Choosing camels, out of all animals, for a drinking comparison could only come from someone who already knew the fact.
