Ants

The Quran described a wingless worker ant speaking in the female form. Modern zoology confirmed that worker ants outside the nest are females.

27:18 · Surah An-Naml
حَتَّىٰ إِذَا أَتَوْا عَلَىٰ وَادِ النَّمْلِ قَالَتْ نَمْلَةٌ يَا أَيُّهَا النَّمْلُ ادْخُلُوا مَسَاكِنَكُمْ
"Until, when they came upon the valley of the ants, a female ant said, 'O ants, enter your homes.'"

The Quran used the female form قَالَتْ for the ant speaking outside the nest. Modern zoology confirmed that worker ants foraging outside the nest are females and do not have wings.
