The Crow Vigils

For centuries, the concept of animals executing ritualistic, instructive actions around their dead was completely unrecorded. The Quran stated it plainly through the behavioral profile of a crow 1400 years before modern biologists discovered it.

5:31 · Surah Al-Ma'idah
فَبَعَثَ اللَّهُ غُرَابًا يَبْحَثُ فِي الْأَرْضِ لِيُرِيَهُ كَيْفَ يُوَارِي سَوْءَةَ أَخِيهِ ۚ قَالَ يَا وَيْلَتَا أَعَجَزْتُ أَنْ أَكُونَ مِثْلَ هَٰذَا الْغُرَابِ
"Then Allah sent a crow digging the ground to show him how to cover his brother's corpse. He said: 'Woe to me! Was I unable to be like this crow?'"
The Discovery
The Quran isolates a crow manipulating the earth to demonstrate mortality management. Modern corvid science has confirmed that crows perform highly structured 'funerals' to cross-examine local threats and protect their communities.
