The Quran uses the word أدنى (adnā) the superlative form meaning "lowest." The battle between the Romans and Persians occurred near the Dead Sea. Modern satellite altimetry reveals that the Dead Sea shore sits at 430 meters below sea level, making it the single lowest land point on the entire planet. This precise geographical fact was only confirmed in the 20th century. No civilization in the 7th century possessed the instruments to measure it.
