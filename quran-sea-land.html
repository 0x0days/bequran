The word البحر (sea) appears 32 times. البر (land) appears 13 times. Their ratio is 71.11 : 28.88 matching Earth's ocean coverage of 70.8% and 29.2% of land ratio.
The Quran was compiled in the 7th century CE. Earth's ocean surface ratio was not scientifically measured until the satellite era. The ratio was not selected, it is the count. No rounding, no adjustment, no choice of which words to include.
